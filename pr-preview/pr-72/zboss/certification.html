ZBOSS library certification

  • Certification levels

  • Certification IDs

Zigbee ZBOSS R23 libraries in the Zigbee R23 add-on for the nRF Connect SDK are always tested in-house by Nordic Semiconductor to conform to Zigbee Pro R23 test specification. However, these libraries are only periodically verified by external test service provider and granted Zigbee Compliant Platform certification by Connectivity Standards Alliance.

Certification levels

The certification is based on CSA’s Zigbee certification policy, which distinguishes the following levels of certification:

  • Compliant Platform

  • Certified Product

The certification guarantees that a platform or product will work and will interoperate with other compliant platforms or certified products, respectively.

Nordic Semiconductor provides the Zigbee Compliant Platform. You can use this platform as the building block for your Zigbee Certified Product, which is conformant with the ZCL and BDB standard.
